# knct

a community of builders working on side projects that turn into real products.

we exchange knowledge, mix engineering and business, and share in what we ship together.

come for the building. if you're here for the money, you'll be disappointed.

## links

- discord: https://discord.gg/tTSsenqhPH
- github: https://github.com/knowledge-circuit
- site: https://knct.dev

## how it works

- members bring side projects, skills, and ideas.
- we collaborate openly — engineering, design, business, distribution.
- contributors share in what we ship together, based on contribution.
- knowledge stays in the community: everything is documented and shared.

## who it's for

builders who want to ship real products with other builders. engineers, designers, operators, domain experts. people who learn by doing and want company while doing it.

not for: people optimizing for short-term cash, passive members, or anyone expecting a job.
